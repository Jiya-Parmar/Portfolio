AGASTYA Rover

An advanced autonomous all-terrain robot designed to navigate challenging environments using a suite of high-precision sensors and technologies. This robot is equipped with GPS for precise location tracking, an Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) for orientation and motion detection, and LiDAR for real-time distance mapping and obstacle detection. Additionally, an IP camera provides live visual feedback, enhancing the robot’s ability to interpret and adapt to its surroundings. Together, these components enable seamless navigation, intelligent decision-making, and safe traversal across a variety of terrains, making it suitable for complex, unstructured environments.

6-DOF Manipulator

A highly capable robotic arm designed for precision tasks, featuring control powered by Arduino and stepper motors. Each joint of the arm is actuated by stepper motors, providing precise, incremental movement and stability, essential for tasks requiring high accuracy. The Arduino microcontroller orchestrates the control signals, enabling smooth operation and allowing for custom programming of complex movements. This setup ensures that the arm can be accurately positioned and controlled for various applications, from pick-and-place operations to more intricate manipulations, making it ideal for both automation and experimental robotics projects.
